Picture Quality

This monitor boasts a 27' screen size, an IPS Panel Type and a Color Gamut 99%(sRGB). The Color Depth is 10bit(8bit + A-FRC) and has a Pixel pitch(mm) of 0.1554x0.1554. Delays are almost non-exsistent with a Response time of 5ms. An Aspect Ratio of 16:9 and a Resolution of 3840x2160 will make every picture crystal clear and the Brightness of Peak 450nits, Typical 350nits, Min 280nits will also help. The Contrast Ratio is 1000:1, Viewing angles are 178/178 (CR>10) and the Surface Treatment is Anti-Glare 3H.
